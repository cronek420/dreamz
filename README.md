# DreamWeaver v3: Your Personal Dream Journal and Analysis Tool

## What is DreamWeaver v3?

DreamWeaver v3 is a digital dream journal that helps you record, understand, and reflect on your dreams. It's a space for you to document your nightly adventures and gain insights into your subconscious mind. With DreamWeaver v3, you can track your dream patterns, analyze their meanings, and explore the rich tapestry of your inner world.

### Key Features:

*   **Dream Journaling:** Easily record your dreams in a clean, intuitive interface.
*   **AI-Powered Dream Analysis:** Get in-depth analysis of your dreams, including themes, symbols, and emotional undertones.
*   **Mood Tracking:** Associate your dreams with specific moods to track your emotional state over time.
*   **AI Art Generation:** Visualize your dreams with AI-generated art that brings your dreamscapes to life.
*   **Shareable Dream Cards:** Create and share beautiful dream cards with your friends and on social media.
*   **Community Insights:** Explore dream trends and patterns from the global community of DreamWeaver users.
*   **Voice-to-Text:** Dictate your dreams for hands-free journaling.
*   **Pro Subscription:** Unlock advanced features, including in-depth dream analysis and unlimited dream entries.

### Mood Selection

After you've written down your dream, you can select a mood that best describes how the dream made you feel. The available moods are:

*   **Happy:** For dreams that left you feeling joyful and content.
*   **Calm:** For peaceful and serene dreams.
*   **Sad:** For dreams that evoked feelings of sadness or loss.
*   **Fearful:** For nightmares or dreams that were unsettling.

### Advanced Analysis Options (Pro Feature)

For users who want to dive deeper into their dreams, DreamWeaver v3 offers advanced analysis options:

*   **Narrative Structure:** This option analyzes the plot and pacing of your dream. It can help you understand the story your subconscious is telling.
*   **Character Archetypes:** Identify common archetypes in your dreams, such as The Mentor, The Shadow, or The Trickster. This can reveal insights into your relationships and personal dynamics.
*   **Recurring Symbols:** Track symbols that appear across multiple dreams. This can help you identify patterns and understand the long-term themes in your dream life.

## Community and Global DreamScape

The Community section of DreamWeaver connects you with a global network of dreamers. This feature is designed to help you understand that you're not alone in your dream experiences and to discover the collective unconscious in a new and exciting way.

### Global Dream Feed

The Global Dream Feed provides a real-time glimpse into the dreams of the community. It's a curated feed of anonymous dream snippets, themes, and moods from around the world. This allows you to see what others are dreaming about, discover common dream themes, and feel a sense of connection with fellow dreamers.

### Explore Trends

Curious about what people are dreaming about in a specific city or region? The Explore Trends feature allows you to ask specific questions about dream patterns. For example, you could ask, "What are people dreaming about in Paris?" and the app will provide insights based on anonymized user data.

## Data Privacy and Anonymity

We take your privacy seriously. All dream data is anonymized and aggregated to protect your identity. When you share your dreams with the community, your personal information is never revealed. This allows you to share your experiences openly and honestly, without fear of judgment.

## Tech Stack

DreamWeaver v3 is built with a modern, robust tech stack to provide a seamless user experience.

*   **Frontend:** The user interface is built with **React** and **TypeScript**, using **Tailwind CSS** for styling. This combination allows for a flexible, responsive, and maintainable design.
*   **Backend:** The backend is powered by **Firebase**, providing a secure and scalable infrastructure for user authentication, database management, and cloud functions.
*   **Dream Analysis:** The dream analysis is powered by **Google's Gemini API**, which provides state-of-the-art natural language processing to interpret the meaning of your dreams.
*   **AI Art Generation:** The AI art generator is also powered by the **Gemini API**, which creates stunning visuals based on the text of your dreams.
*   **Speech Recognition:** The voice-to-text feature is implemented using the browser's built-in **Web Speech API**.
